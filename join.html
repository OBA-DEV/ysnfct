Classification

Young Surveyors are those that meet the FIG definiton of a Young Surveyor: Surveyors aged 35 years or under, Students of surveying and all related studies, Young Professionals of surveying and all related studies within 10 years of graduation from a Bachelor or Masters degree. Supporters are anyone interested in furthering the mission of the Young Surveyors group.
